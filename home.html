About the place

Belgrave lakes is one of the many lakes located with the town of tamworth,

The lakes are a collection of smaller lakes with a variety of different activites and areas for the community to use and enjoy,

These range from a outdoor gym to a BMX track.

It is frequented by dog walkers and families with children due to its peaceful pathways and wide variety of animals.

The area surrounding the lakes and all of the activies are provided and maintained by the tamworth borough council.
